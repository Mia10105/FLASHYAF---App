The Solution

Support, built for the moment it's happening.

FLASHYAF™ is a real-time wellness companion — live tracking, personalized insight, and a calmer way through the flash, from start to finish.

← Back to home

Live, in-the-moment tracking

When a flash starts, you tell FLASHYAF™ — and it stays with you through it. The app tracks duration and intensity across the stages of an episode, from onset to cooldown, so you have a clear picture of what just happened the moment it's over instead of trying to reconstruct it later.

Personalized over time

Every flash you log adds to your own history — and FLASHYAF™ uses that history to recognize your patterns: when flashes tend to happen, how long they last, and what seems to help. The more you log, the better it gets to know you. This is a historical summary of your own data only — it's not used to predict or guess what's coming next.

A real screen from the FLASHYAF™ app — live tracking from start to Emergency Cool Down.

Where you feel it

FLASHYAF™ lets you mark exactly where a flash is showing up — face, neck, chest, back, arms, or lower body — building a fuller picture of your experience over time, not just a generic log entry.

A calmer way through it

Beyond tracking, FLASHYAF™ brings in light, supportive tools — including Humor Bank™ and Grounding Support™, available anytime through the Emergency Cool Down option — to help you ride out a flash with a little less stress in the moment.

Private by design

Your data is yours. FLASHYAF™ is built with privacy and security as a foundation, not an afterthought.

What this is — and isn't

FLASHYAF™ is a General Wellness product. It's designed to help you track and understand your own experience — it does not diagnose, treat, or replace medical care. For any health concerns, please talk to a healthcare provider.
