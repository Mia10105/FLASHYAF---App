Pioneer Beta Program

Be one of the first 100 to shape FLASHYAF™.

A free two-week trial, founding-member pricing, and a real voice in what gets built next.

← Back to home

Why become a Pioneer Beta Member?

  • Try it free for two weeks before deciding anything — no charge, no pressure.
  • Lock in founding-member pricing for 24 months from launch — GROW for $9.99/mo, FLOW for $19.99/mo, well below standard rates.
  • Shape the product directly — bi-weekly feedback (about 10 minutes every 2 weeks) goes straight into what we build next.
  • Early access to every new feature before anyone else sees it.
  • A real voice in a community being built with its members, not just for them.

What we ask of Pioneer Beta Members

  • Log a minimum of 8 active sessions per month (logins, flashes, or community posts — any combination)
  • Submit bi-weekly feedback (~10 minutes every 2 weeks)
  • Log real flash data only

🔒 A reminder on privacy

Your personal data — name, email, account details, flash session data — is kept in a separate, encrypted environment from any optional research data. Your voice notes stay on your device via Memory Vault™ and never reach our servers. If you ever opt into a research study, only an anonymous identifier is shared — never your name or other personally identifiable information, and you can withdraw that consent at any time with no effect on your account or pricing. Read the full Privacy Policy for details.

If you decide it's not for you

Life happens, and that's okay. Here's exactly what to expect:

If you...What happens
Cancel and return within 90 daysYour Pioneer rate is fully restored — no new buy-in
Return after 90 days, but within 2 years of launch$14.99/mo GROW or $24.99/mo FLOW — a new buy-in is required
Return after 2 years from launchStandard public pricing applies

Before any cancellation is finalized, we'll offer you a one-month pause instead — your rate and your data both stay intact. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation so you can export it any time during that window; after that, it's permanently deleted. Your two-week trial buy-in itself is final and non-refundable once completed — but nothing stops you from pausing or returning later under the terms above.

At the end of your 24-month Pioneer rate lock, your plan simply rolls over to standard public pricing — you'll get a 30-day advance notice before that happens, with no surprises.
