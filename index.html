The Problem

Menopause Doesn't Wait.
The Support Isn't There Yet.

1B+

Women worldwide are in menopause-related transitions or postmenopause.

Source: global menopause population estimates.

Up to 80%

May experience hot flashes or night sweats during menopause-related transitions.

Source: menopause vasomotor symptom literature.

In the Moment

Support is needed while symptoms are happening, not only after they are logged.

General wellness support only.

Current solutions often lean toward after-the-fact logging or static education. FLASHYAF™ is built for the moment a user wants support during a hot flash episode.

The founder's own logged experience showed a personal pattern between episodes — founder-specific data only, not a universal claim. That lived experience inspired a tool that helps each user view her own past logged patterns, without forecasting, countdowns, diagnosis, or treatment claims.

The Solution

Real-Time Support, Stage By Stage.

FLASHYAF™ follows a simple logged stage flow — Start, Peak, End, and Back to Normal — offering hands-free behavioral coping support when a user chooses to activate it.

Stage 1 — Start

The episode begins. Support activates by voice or tap.

Stage 2 — Peak

The user can access in-the-moment coping support.

Stage 3 — End

The episode is logged as complete.

Stage 4 — Back To Normal

The user can reset, breathe, and return to the moment.

"I wasn't looking for help with my period. I was looking for help with menopause-related transitions. What I couldn't find was real-time support. So I decided to build it myself."

— Iva Marie Brown-Ziegler, Founder & CEO, FLASHYAF™

Iva Marie Brown-Ziegler founded BROWNWORKS4U2™ LLC and built FLASHYAF™ from the ground up — product design, brand, system architecture, and day-to-day operations — driven by a gap in real-time menopause support that she experienced firsthand and could not find solved anywhere on the market. As a Black woman founder building in women's health, she brings both lived experience and hands-on execution to a category that has been chronically underserved and under-built for the women living through it.

FAQ

Questions, Answered.

Is FLASHYAF™ a medical device?+

No. FLASHYAF™ is a General Wellness support platform. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.

Does it tell me when my next hot flash will happen?+

No. Flash History Insight™ shows historical patterns from your own logged data only — past intervals and averages. It does not forecast, count down to, or claim to prevent future episodes.

What's the Pioneer Beta Program?+

A limited program for the first 100 members to help shape FLASHYAF™ before public launch, with a Pioneer rate for 24 months from public launch.

Is my data private?+

FLASHYAF™ is designed around user control and consent. We do not use health-screen activity for advertising, and voice notes are designed to remain local to your device unless you choose to share or submit them.

When does FLASHYAF™ launch?+

Pioneer Beta opens to the first 100 members in Q3–Q4 2026, ahead of public launch. Join the waitlist to be notified the moment access opens.

Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Control.

Who we are

BROWNWORKS4U2™ LLC operates FLASHYAF™, a voice-first General Wellness support platform. Woman-owned, minority-owned, based in Virginia, USA.

What we collect

Account details, flash session timestamps and duration, and device information used for security, troubleshooting, and product improvement. Payments are processed by Stripe; FLASHYAF™ does not store full card numbers.

Voice notes and microphone use

Memory Vault™ voice notes are designed to remain local to your device unless you choose to share or submit them. Microphone access is designed to be user-activated and limited by a short timeout window.

What we don't do

We do not use health-screen activity for advertising, and we do not allow advertising pixels on private health-support screens.

Research is always separate and optional

If you opt into a research study, only an anonymous identifier is used — never your name or email. Participation is voluntary, revocable anytime in Settings, and never required to use FLASHYAF™.

Your rights

Access, correction, deletion requests, data portability, and the ability to withdraw research consent or opt out of marketing at any time. Contact customersupport@flashyafapp.com to exercise any right.

FLASHYAF™ is a General Wellness support platform. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider regarding menopause-related transitions or any medical condition. In case of emergency, call 911.
