Do I get a refund if I'm unsatisfied?

You can cancel anytime, and your access continues through the end of your current billing period — we don't issue partial-month refunds for time already used. Pioneer Beta Members get a free two-week trial before any buy-in is ever charged, so you can fully test FLASHYAF™ before committing. Once a Pioneer buy-in is completed, it's final and non-refundable, though you can still pause or cancel afterward under our standard cancellation terms. Full details are on the Pricing page and the Beta Tester page.
