Privacy Policy

Nothing hidden in small print.

Everything here is written in plain language so you understand exactly what happens with your data.

← Back to home

Who we are

BROWNWORKS4U2™ LLC operates FLASHYAF™, a voice-first, real-time menopause General Wellness support platform. Woman-owned, minority-owned, based in Virginia, USA.

Business: imbz4u2@proton.me  |  Support: customersupport@flashyafapp.com
Address: 3214 Electric Rd PMB 1122 Ste 102, Roanoke, VA 24018

What data we collect

  • Account: Name, email address, age range, menopause-related transition stage
  • Flash session data: Stage timestamps, intensity, duration, voice command inputs
  • Voice notes: Captured locally on your device via Memory Vault™. Not transmitted to our servers — yours only.
  • Device info: App version, OS, device type (for bug resolution only)
  • Payment: Processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card numbers.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your personal health data with advertisers.
  • We do not allow third-party tracking pixels on any health interaction screen.
  • We do not share health data through referral URLs.
  • We do not passively listen through your microphone at any time. The microphone activates only when you initiate a voice command, with a 30-second hard timeout (Mic Privacy Lock™) that automatically closes it after each command.

How we protect your privacy

Your personal information — name, email, account details, flash session data — lives in a separate, encrypted environment from any optional research data. Your voice notes stay on your device via Memory Vault™ and never reach our servers. If you opt into a research study, only an anonymous identifier is shared — never your name, email, or other personally identifiable information. No data moves between these environments without your affirmative, explicit consent.

Data breach notification

FLASHYAF™ operates as a Vendor of Personal Health Records under the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule (16 CFR Part 318). In the event of a breach affecting 500+ users, we will notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay and no later than 60 days after discovery, and will notify the FTC at the same time.

Your data after cancellation

Your account data is retained for 90 days after cancellation, during which you can export your complete flash history. After 90 days, it is permanently deleted. Any anonymous research data you contributed cannot be linked back to you and remains in an isolated research database for the duration of the approved study only.

Pioneer Member pricing window

Pioneer Member pricing is locked for exactly 24 calendar months from the public launch date. This is not a lifetime rate. Existing subscribers receive advance notice before any pricing change and may cancel before it takes effect, without penalty.

Research opt-in

Research participation is always voluntary, requires separate consent from your subscription, and is revocable at any time in Settings with no effect on your account or pricing.

Your rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion (processed within 30 days)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw research consent at any time
  • Right to opt out of marketing (unsubscribe link in every email)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at customersupport@flashyafapp.com or imbz4u2@proton.me.

Medical disclaimer

FLASHYAF™ is a General Wellness support platform. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider regarding menopause-related transitions or any medical condition. In case of emergency, call 911.
