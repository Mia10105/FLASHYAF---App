Refund & cancellation policy

You can cancel anytime — your access continues through the end of your current billing period, and we don't do partial-month refunds for time already used. Pioneer Beta Members get a free two-week trial before any buy-in is charged; once that buy-in is completed, it's final and non-refundable, though you can still pause or cancel your subscription afterward (see the full terms on the Beta Tester page). If pricing ever changes, we'll always notify you at least 30 days in advance, and you're guaranteed to keep your current rate for the rest of that billing cycle plus one additional full month before any new price applies.

What this is — and isn't

FLASHYAF™ is a General Wellness product. Pricing covers app features and support tools only — it is not a medical service, and no tier includes diagnosis, treatment, or clinical care. For medical concerns, please talk to a healthcare provider.
